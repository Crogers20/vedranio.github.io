My work

I believe that design has more in common with science and engineering than art. Art is subjective, design is not – therefore, it is important for designers to be able to articulate their decisions through a coherent design process.

Here is a selection of some of my work, illustrating my design process.

Visitor registration app

Visitor registration app

I designed a customisable visitor registration iPad app, specifically for gyms and fitness centres.

The app is about to be deployed across 100s of gyms in the US.

Credit card payment form

I redesigned the credit card payment form used during job ad posting.

The redesign caused a 45% increase in customers paying upfront (rather than by invoice), resulting in a six figure increase in upfront cash flow.

Workshop facilitation and wireframe design

I ran a two day design workshop for a development team in Sarajevo, and their Dutch clients, followed by wireframe designs for the entire product.

As a result, both the clients and the dev team were involved in the design process and everyone had a clear idea on how to proceed with the implementation.
