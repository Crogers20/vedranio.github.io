About me

I am a designer with over 10 years' experience, focusing on User Experience (UX) design, usability and interface design.

I am currently working as a Senior UX Designer at Seek.

Technical beginnings

After graduating from Engineering (robotics) and Computer Science in 2004, I started my career as a system design engineer working on automated cancer diagnostics instruments. I developed a passion for observing how people interact with the machines, systems and software I worked on.

A turning point

A career shaping event was an opportunity to spend two years working in the UK and across Europe, studying how medical staff use our instruments and helping them improve their processes.

That was in 2006. That passion for observing how people interact with products and improving their experiences lives on in me and is the key reason why I love what I do.
